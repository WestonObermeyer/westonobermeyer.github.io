there are just a few systems i think everyone should know about before speaking idenesko. this is a page containing miscellaneous idenesk systems which don't fit into the grammar page (because the grammar page is generally intended for sentence structure), or any other pages.

first and most importantly: plurals. plurals are simple, because you can add an -n suffix to any word which ends in a vowel. and yes, this does mean that verbs can't be made into plurals - you must first remove the tense ending. this process essentially makes the verb a noun and then pluralizes it.
