Tenses (Tensen) Ppast: add the phrase "'ad" as a suffix after the verb in the sentence. present: leave as-is. future: add the phrase "'ent" as a suffix after the verb in the sentence. Example: i was (is eim'ad) i am (is eim) i will be (is eim'ent) A unique feature of Westonian is that the words "ad" and "ent" used to describe past and future tenses can also be used to mean things like "ago".
