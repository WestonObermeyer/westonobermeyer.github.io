westonian is a language of my invention. i purposely designed it to be simple,
following a strict but minimal set of rules. i strongly encourage users of the
language to create their own words and dialects in a way that ensures that
the language's rules are being honored, and the meaning of every word and sentence
are being retained. there are multiple ways to create new words, including:
-adapting words from other languages
-compounding existing westonian words
-creating new words from scratch

To keep things phonetically simple, the majority of westonian letters are pronounced
just as they are in English. However, the rules for pronounciation are intended to
be more strict in that not a single word can be the exception to a standing rule.
for example, in english, there are a lot of letters (like g, e or c) that are pronounced
differently depending on where they are in the sentence (take the 'silent E' as an example).

that being said, the growth and future of the language is essentially out of my hands,
meaning it's the responsibility of the user to speak it however they like. word order,
accents and slang are up to you; the best thing i can reccomend, however, is to make it
make sense. you CAN mix up the words in a sentence to make the meaning of the sentence
totally unclear, but you're most contributing to the growth of the language by
speaking in a way that makes it easy to understand.
